Care and Services

HSC offers more than just care providers, we also offer a "quality of life component," where this phase of every senior resident's life is seen as significant. Making this time beneficial and enjoyable is our primary objective.

Part of the mission of HSC is to enhance and extend the quality of life of our residents through our of healthy meals menu, which is prepared by our specially trained cooks. Our menu is prepared by using organic culinary recipes, as planned by our dietary/nutrition consultant.

HSC offers various activities and an art program workshop, with many different mediums available to bring out the creative side of our residents.

Caregiving services include...

  • Assistance with medical orders for daily care.
  • Bathing, dressing and grooming assistance.
  • Dental and oral hygiene care.
  • Assist residents with transfers to different areas of home.
  • Assistance with ambulation.
  • Medication reminders
  • Meal planning, including for special dietary needs.
  • Housekeeping and laundry services.
  • Advocate for the residents well-being.
  • Coordination with the family and residents.
