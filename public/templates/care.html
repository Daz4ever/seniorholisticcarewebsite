Care and Services

HSC offers more than just skilled care providers, we also offer "quality of life component," where this phase of every senior resident's life is seen as important; and how to make this time beneficial and enjoyable is our primary objective.

Part of the mission of HSC is to enhance and extend the quality of life of our residents through our healthy meals menu which is prepared by our specially trained cooks. Our menu is prepared by using organic culinary recipes as planned by our dietary/nutrition expert consultants.

HSC offers an art program with many different mediums available to bring out the creative side of our residents.

Caregiving services include...

  • Assistance with medical orders for daily care.
  • Bathing, dressing and grooming assistance.
  • Dental and oral hygiene care.
  • Assist patient with transference.
  • Assistance with amulation
  • Medication reminders
  • Meal planning, including for special dietary needs.
  • Housekeeping and laundry services.
  • Advocate for the clients well-being.
  • Coordination with the family and residents.
