About Us

Holistic Senior Care, LLC (HSC) was created to address the growing number of seniors seeking quality affordable long term care in a place they can truly call home.

HSC provides a luxurious standard of living for seniors who want to enjoy their retirement years.

HSC was founded on the belief that seniors do not have to settle for low quality housing and care.
